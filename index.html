TechInnovation in Enterprise 2.0

🌟 Students' Katta #13 : Tech Innovation in Enterprise 2.0 🌟

Inspiration and Insight — All in one day!

With 20 diverse participants, the event brought together a mix of budding entrepreneurs—including a Class XII student—alongside engineering, design, and MBA students. Mentorship came from seasoned professionals, including the Head of an International Office at a leading university and a veteran people strategist. It was a blend of perspectives and personalities—the very essence of what defines a Validus EduTech #VET experience.

✨ Highlights of the Key Session by Neeraj Athalye:

  • 💡 Guide to Company Value Chain
  • 🔍 Innovation Through a Financial Lens
  • 📊 How Enterprises Think in Numbers
  • 💼 Where Does Your Work Show Up in the Balance Sheet?
  • 🚀 From Ideas to Impact: Financial Thinking for Innovators
  • 📎 The Business Side of Innovation
  • 💸 Follow the Money: Cash Flow Awareness

7 July 2025

INTRAPRENEURSHIP

🌟 Students’ Katta #12 : INTRAPRENEURSHIP 🌟

“Intrapreneurs are dreamers who do. Those who take responsibility for creating an innovation of any kind within an organization.” – Gifford Pinchot III (1985)

  • ▪️ Session 1: Ms. Suhani Kolhatkar (Co-founder & Community Director) opened the event with greetings, an orientation on ValidusEduTech, and the flow of the day.
  • ▪️ Session 2: Mr. Anand Khandekar (Co-founder) spoke on Taking Initiative—sharing real stories of students & employees who grew by going beyond assigned tasks.
  • ▪️ Session 3: Mrs. Alpana Phalke Thakar (VP Operations, Securly Software) highlighted Value Creation and engaged in a lively Q&A with participants.
  • ▪️ Interactive Activity: Participants explored traits like #Adaptability, #Innovation, and #InfluenceWithoutAuthority through a hands-on case study with Toy Trunk Pvt. Ltd.
  • ▪️ Networking: Thanks to Acube Studio (Ecour Studio) for providing a perfect mix of conference room & terrace space for collaboration.
  • ▪️ Team Challenge: Groups presented solutions to revive Toy Trunk’s business—showcasing unique ideas and true intrapreneurial spirit.
